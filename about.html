About Us

ABC CBSE School is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering holistic development in students.

Our faculty comprises experienced and highly qualified teachers who are passionate about teaching and committed to nurturing the potential of each student.

We offer a diverse and comprehensive curriculum that includes a wide range of subjects, from sciences and mathematics to humanities and arts, ensuring a well-rounded education.

Our state-of-the-art science and computer laboratories provide students with hands-on learning experiences, encouraging them to explore and innovate.

In addition to academics, we place a strong emphasis on extracurricular activities, including sports, music, art, and drama, to help students develop their talents and interests.

We believe in creating a supportive and inclusive environment where every student feels valued and respected. Our school culture promotes kindness, empathy, and collaboration.

Our students have consistently excelled in various competitions and events at regional, national, and international levels, bringing laurels to the school.

We are committed to preparing our students for global citizenship by instilling in them a sense of responsibility, ethical values, and a passion for lifelong learning.

Our campus is designed to provide a conducive learning environment, with green spaces, modern classrooms, and facilities that support both academic and extracurricular pursuits.

We actively engage with the community through various outreach programs and initiatives, encouraging students to contribute positively to society.

At ABC CBSE School, we prioritize the well-being and mental health of our students, offering counseling services and support to help them navigate challenges and achieve their goals.

Our History and Offerings

Since 1982, ABC School has specialized in teaching the Italian language to foreign students. Situated in the heart of Florence, very close to Ponte Vecchio, the Piazza Santa Croce, and the Piazza della Signoria, it is in one of the oldest and liveliest parts of the city of Florence: a perfect base for those who would like to explore the historic center.

We have light and airy classrooms, some of which have frescoed ceilings and panoramic views. From one side of the school, you can see the fascinating and quiet via dei Bardi, and from the other and from our terrace, Palazzo Vecchio, the Cathedral, Ponte Vecchio, the Uffizi Gallery, and the river Arno.

All of our linguistic programs give a lot of importance to culture and art as an integrated part of students’ learning through seminars or guided visits taken by our teachers, which usually take place in the afternoon or during the day on Saturdays when the school is closed.

Our principal objective is to offer our students the possibility to study Italian whilst discovering the culture and beauty of both Italy and Tuscany for both personal and professional growth.
